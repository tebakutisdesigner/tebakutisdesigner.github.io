Mjolnir: Hammer of Tempered Elements - Design Diary

This video takes you through the first level of my Guildhall Team Project Mjolnir: Hammer of Tempered Elements. I explain why I designed this level the way I did, how I think about game design, and what I intended to teach the player about our game as they move through it. I was the lead programmer on this project and programmed all gameplay and UI elements you see in this video using Torque Script, including the UI, environmental objects (such as elemental braziers and doors), monster pathing, monster AI (including pursuit), physics (moving, jumping, rushing), melee and ranged attacks, unique level elements (the freezing waterfall), and damage tracking for players and monsters.
